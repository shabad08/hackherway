Smart Insights
Recommendations based on price, production, and trade data
Wheat — Strong selling window
Mandi prices are at MSP (₹2,275/q). Production is up 2.7% but export demand remains strong at $2.1B. Current margins are healthy. Ideal to sell 60–70% of stock now and hold rest for 3–4 weeks.
Rice — Caution: prices slightly below MSP in some mandis
Some markets show rice prices ₹80–100 below MSP of ₹2,183/q. Production up 3.3% YoY adds supply pressure. Wait for government procurement (PMGKAY) to begin in your district before selling if possible.
Cotton — Risk zone: production down, prices uncertain
Cotton production fell 7.5% due to pest pressure. However, international prices are also soft. MSP is ₹5,800/q but many mandis are trading below this. Strongly consider selling to CCI if available in your district.
Mustard — Excellent outlook
Prices are ₹250 above MSP at ₹5,450/q. Export demand for edible oils strong. Production up 6.8%. This is a profitable crop with good export linkage. Farmers can sell freely in open market.
Gram — Recovery underway
Despite prices being slightly below MSP, production increase of 9.2% signals improved supply. Government buffer stocking likely. Prices expected to firm up in Q2 2025. Consider short-term storage if you have capacity.
